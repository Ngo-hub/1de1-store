ABOUT 1DE1

1DE1 is a limited edition streetwear brand built different. Every piece is numbered. Every drop is final. No restocks. No compromises.

We operate under Play NGO Systems — a creative operation founded by Jeffrey and Darielis. 1DE1 doesn't follow trends. It drops when it's ready.

Uni 001 is the first drop. Three pieces. Each one limited. The designs are mixed media artwork created by Darielis — cut and assembled by hand from vintage magazines. No computer. No shortcuts. Every graphic is one of one before the clothing even exists.

The game store at 1de1.shop isn't just a gimmick. It's how we think about drops — as experiences, not transactions. You pick your character. You try on the fit. You cop or you don't.

There won't be another Uni 001. That's the point.
